Pin Assignments for Wemos D1 Mini:
OLED Display (I2C):

SCL → D1 (GPIO5).
SDA → D2 (GPIO4).

Other Components:
Tempo Button → Reassign to D3 (GPIO0).
Plus Button → Reassign to D5 (GPIO14).
Minus Button → Reassign to D6 (GPIO12).
MIDI Output → Keep on D7 (GPIO13).
LED → Connect to a free pin like D4 (GPIO2).

Wiring Diagram Overview:
Component	Pin on Wemos D1 Mini	Other Connection
OLED SCL (Clock)	D1 (GPIO5)	SCL on OLED Display
OLED SDA (Data)	D2 (GPIO4)	SDA on OLED Display
LED	D4 (GPIO2)	GND (via 220Ω resistor)
Tempo Button	D3 (GPIO0)	GND
Plus Button	D5 (GPIO14)	GND
Minus Button	D6 (GPIO12)	GND
MIDI Output Pin 5	D7 (GPIO13)	Pin 5 on MIDI DIN Connector (via 220Ω resistor)
MIDI Output Pin 4	5V	Pin 4 on MIDI DIN Connector (via 220Ω resistor)
MIDI Output Pin 2	GND	Pin 2 on MIDI DIN Connector
